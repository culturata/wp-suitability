How It Works

From content creation to brand-safe publishing in three simple steps.

01

Write & Create

Create your content in the WordPress Gutenberg editor or Classic Editor as you normally would. Our plugin integrates seamlessly without disrupting your workflow.

02

Analyze & Review

Click "Analyze" in the sidebar. Our AI evaluates your content across 12 GARM risk categories, assigns an overall score (0-100), and provides detailed sentiment analysis and IAB categorization.

03

Apply & Publish

Review actionable recommendations with side-by-side text comparisons. Apply fixes with one click, then publish with confidence knowing your content meets brand safety standards.
